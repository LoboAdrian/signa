Who we are

Building
Perfection

Signa Construction


is a boutique, fully licensed and insured general contractor company specializing in high-end remodeling, renovation, and new construction projects.

With over 10 years of experience, Signa Construction provides to its customers the highest quality results in each of their projects, recognizing the needs of their clients and safety of their investments.

Signa Construction demands excellence from sub-contractors and our own team. It is our business to provide competitive pricing, supreme quality, and on-time delivery. Our commitment and attention to detail is unparalleled.

We have earned the prestige of working with exclusive groups of customers in the US, Mexico, Brazil, Venezuela, Germany, and Panama, among others. In addition, our company has established excellent working relationships with well-known designers from all over the world.
